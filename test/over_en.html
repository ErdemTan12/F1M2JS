About us

About us

Rijksmuseum is a dutch national museum dedicated to art and history in Amsterdam. The museum is located on the Museumplein in the south of the city near the Concertgebouw and Stedelijk Museum Amsterdam.

What we do

Rijksmuseum is the museum of the Netherlands. We tell the story of 800 years of Dutch history starting the year 1200 until now. Besides that we organize multiple expositions every year with our own collections and pieces from other museums.
