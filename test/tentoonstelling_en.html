High Skies

The coming years, 4 expositions with pieces of our collection will travel through all museums. We have already started our third collection called the Hoge Luchten. Like the first two expositions, 'Lage landen' and 'Koele Wateren', this exposition is based on one of the four elements, air. 40 landscapes and faces show the iconic Dutch skies.
